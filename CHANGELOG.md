# Changelog

## [1.0.0] - 2026-03-25
### Added
- Initial release of Volume Fading.
- Smooth volume reduction logic (Foreground Service).
- Custom background with night sea photography.
- Quick presets for 15, 30, and 60 minutes.
- Multi-language support (EN, RU, UK, ES).

### Fixed
- Fixed app icon on Xiaomi/Redmi devices (MIUI/HyperOS).
- Fixed background service stability on Android 10+.
- Disabled Impeller for better performance on older devices.
