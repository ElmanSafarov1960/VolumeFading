# Privacy Policy for Volume Fading

**Last updated: March 25, 2026**

Elman ("we", "our", or "us") operates the **Volume Fading** mobile application.

### 1. Data Collection
We do **not** collect, store, or share any personal information. 
* No registration is required.
* No analytics or tracking services are used.
* No location data is accessed.

### 2. Permissions Used
The app requires specific permissions to function correctly:
* **MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS:** Needed only to decrease the device volume during the fade process.
* **FOREGROUND_SERVICE:** Required to keep the fading process active while the screen is off or the app is in the background.
* **POST_NOTIFICATIONS:** Used to show the service status in the notification bar (required for Android 13+).

### 3. Third-Party Services
The app does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

### 4. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub.
